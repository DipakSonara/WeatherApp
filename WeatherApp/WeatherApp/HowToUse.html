Weather App

Weather, everybody wants to know how it is going to be during the week. Will it be rainy, windy, or sunny? Luckily for us, in the information age, there are open APIs to retrieve information about it.

For this assignment you will be using the API from: http://openweathermap.org/api. The API key is provided at the end of the statement, or you can request your own by registering on the website for free.

How to use app:

  • By tapping on Search Field at home screen, you can type any city name.
  • According to your search criteria, you will have option matched with your text.
  • A list suggesting places name will be auto populated below search field
  • By tapping on any of the suggestion, you can get the weather information of that place.
  • Home screen will display weather information for all the places you searched. 
  • By tapping on list on home screen, you can see detailed information about weather in detail screen.
  • At the home screen, you have option at top left that will navigate you to this help screen.
  • At the home screen, you have option at top right that will navigate you to map screen with all the locations of searched places on map.
